# User Guide: Managing Your AI Shopping Assistant

Welcome to your new AI-powered storefront assistant! This guide will help you understand how the agent works and how you can manage it for your Shopify store.

## What Your Assistant Can Do

Your assistant is trained to help customers with:
- **Product Discovery**: "Show me some red sneakers" or "What's the best gift for a teenager?"
- **Order Tracking**: "Where is my order #1234?"
- **Policy Questions**: "What is your return policy?" or "Do you ship to Canada?"
- **Cart Management**: "Add this to my cart" or "Increase the quantity of the hat in my cart."

## Setting Up for Success

To get the best results from your AI, follow these steps in your Shopify Admin:

1.  **Update Your Policies**: Go to *Settings > Policies*. Ensure your Refund, Privacy, Shipping, and Terms of Service policies are clearly written. The AI reads these to answer customer questions.
2.  **Organize Your Products**: Ensure your product titles, descriptions, and tags are accurate. Use high-quality images, as the AI will display these in the chat.
3.  **Create an FAQ Page**: Under *Online Store > Pages*, create an FAQ page. The AI uses this as a knowledge base.

## Monitoring Conversations

(Note: This feature depends on your agency's implementation)
- You can review chat logs to see common questions your customers are asking.
- This data is invaluable for identifying "gaps" in your product descriptions or policies.

## Handling Customer Privacy

- The AI is secure. It only accesses a customer's private order information after they securely log in via Shopify's official login page.
- We never store customer passwords.

## When the AI Can't Help

If a customer's request is too complex, the AI is programmed to:
1.  Apologize for the limitation.
2.  Provide your store's contact information.
3.  Suggest that a human representative will follow up.

## Customizing the Look

The chat bubble can be styled to match your brand's colors and logo. Contact your developer to update the `chat.css` file for a seamless look.
